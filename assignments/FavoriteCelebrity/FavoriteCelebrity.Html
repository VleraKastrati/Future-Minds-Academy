Adele's History

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins (born 5 May 1988) is an English singer, songwriter, musician and multi-instrumentalist.

She graduated from The BRIT School in Croydon in May 2006.

In July 2006, Adele published two songs in the fourth issue of online arts magazine PlatformsMagazine.com.

Career

Adele's first single released was "Hometown Glory".

In 2011 Adele released her second album, 21, named after her age at the time it was made.

It was number one in more than 26 countries. It became the biggest selling album of the 21st century.

Personal Life

She has a son named Angelo, with her then-boyfriend, Simon Konecki, born in October 19, 2012.

In 2017, Adele confirmed that she is married to Konecki. In April 2019, it was reported that the two ended their marriage.

My Favorite Song

My favorite song of Adele is "Set Fire to the Rain".

No proper music video was shot for "Set Fire to the Rain" (as well as other singles from 21, "Rumour Has It" and "Turning Tables") as Adele had recently undergone vocal surgery.

Instead, a live performance video filmed at The Royal Albert Hall was uploaded on YouTube to her Vevo account and has received more than 789 million views as of December 2023.
