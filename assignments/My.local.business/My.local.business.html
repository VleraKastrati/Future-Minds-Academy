Planet's History

Resort Planet is a business that operates a family restaurant and a hotel in the village of Kmetovc in Gjilan.

The business is jointly owned by a woman entrepreneur.

The resort is located in a rural area and employs people from the local community.

Location of Planet

The resort's location and services offer customers a unique way to experience the nature of Kosovo.
